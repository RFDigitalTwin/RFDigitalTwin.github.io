Welcome to **RF Digital Twin** - The RF Digital Twin platform for next-generation wireless system simulation and optimization.

## What is RF Digital Twin?

RF Digital Twin is a cutting-edge simulation platform that creates high-fidelity digital twins of wireless communication systems. By combining physics-based ray tracing, electromagnetic modeling, and machine learning, RF Digital Twin enables researchers and engineers to:

- **Design** and optimize wireless networks before deployment
- **Predict** real-world performance with unprecedented accuracy
- **Accelerate** development cycles through rapid prototyping
- **Reduce** costs by minimizing physical testing

## Key Capabilities

### 🌊 Physics-Based Simulation
- Accurate electromagnetic wave propagation modeling
- Material-aware ray tracing with reflection, diffraction, and scattering
- Support for complex indoor and outdoor environments

### 🎯 Multi-Technology Support
- **5G/6G Networks**: mmWave and sub-6GHz bands
- **Radar Systems**: FMCW, pulse, and MIMO configurations
- **WiFi/Bluetooth**: Indoor positioning and connectivity
- **Satellite Communications**: LEO, MEO, and GEO constellations

### ⚡ Real-Time Performance
- GPU-accelerated computation for instant results
- Parallel processing of millions of rays
- Interactive visualization and analysis

### 🔄 End-to-End Differentiability
- Gradient-based optimization of system parameters
- Machine learning integration for intelligent design
- Automatic differentiation through the entire signal chain

## Why Choose RF Digital Twin?

### Industry-Leading Accuracy
Our proprietary ray tracing engine achieves:
- **< 3 dB** mean prediction error in urban environments
- **95%** correlation with real-world measurements
- **Sub-centimeter** spatial resolution
